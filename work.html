Research Projects
Data Mining on Construction Sites10/2021 - 09/2024

Funded by Innovations Management Bau GmbH

A collaborative research project that explored new approaches to acquiring and processing construction site data. We developed deep learning–based methods to transform raw visual and sensor inputs into higher-level information, addressing the lack of transparency in construction processes. The outcomes contribute to more data-driven and informed decision-making in construction management.
